Ecclesiastes 8:9 — “Man has dominated man to his injury.”

The Domination Leakage Model
Power & Preservation
Leaders and systems create policies to protect status and control. Driven by fear of loss, institutional bias, and status protection.
Political & Ideological Alignment
Politics becomes identity. Confirmation bias and in-group vs out-group thinking shape division.
Cultural Domination
Domination becomes subtle. Workplace cliques, resistance to difference, identity protection.
Survival & Internalized Domination
Even those with little power seek control somewhere. Family dynamics, schools, bullying — the cycle continues.

Leadership Disruption

Domination is not just structural — it is cyclical and scalable. As leaders, our role is not to deny it, but to interrupt the cycle through mentorship, fairness, awareness, and intentional inclusion.
